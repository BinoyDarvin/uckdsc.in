HELLO WORLD!

UCK developer student club was implemented with the sole aim of improving coding skills among students as well increasing interest in software development and related fields. The club comprising of students from the University College of Engineering aims to develop profiles of the students with respect to their coding skills in order to enhance their chances of securing a successful career. The college also benefits as a result of the publicity obtained from conducting multiple events by industry leaders

How does it work?

The club works by "Learning through doing". Our portfolio includes ideas from students as well as outsourced projects.
